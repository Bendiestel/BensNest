# Welcome to the new Nest!

This post is stored as a **markdown file** in the `src/posts` directory.

## How to add new posts

1. Create a new `.md` file in `src/posts`.
2. Add the metadata block at the top:
   ```yaml
   ---
   title: Your Title
   date: YYYY-MM-DD
   image: URL (optional)
   ---
   ```
3. Write your content below!

You can use *italics*, **bold**, lists, and even [links](https://google.com).

Enjoy your static blog!
